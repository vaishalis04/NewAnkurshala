English Literature Course

Welcome to the English Literature course! This course explores classic and modern texts, key themes, characters, and literary techniques. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, this course will enhance your understanding and appreciation of English literature.

Course Outline

  • Introduction to English Literature
  • Exploring Literary Genres: Poetry, Drama, Fiction
  • Key Themes in Literature: Love, Conflict, Identity
  • Study of Major Works: Shakespeare, Jane Austen, Charles Dickens
  • Literary Criticism and Analysis
